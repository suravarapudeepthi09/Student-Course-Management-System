Project Contains 3 Roles


Admin



Responsible for overseeing the project and making.


Faculty



Provides guidance and expertise to the students.

Student



Follow guidance and expertise from the faculty.

   

Admin

-  

The Admin module of the Student Course Management System allows administrators to manage various aspects of the system, including user accounts, courses, enrollments, and system settings. Administrators have the highest level of access and can perform tasks such as adding new users, assigning roles, and generating reports.

   

Faculty

-  

The Faculty module enables instructors to manage their courses, upload course materials, communicate with students, and evaluate student performance. Faculty members can view their course rosters, post announcements, grade assignments, and provide feedback to students.

   

Student

-  

The Student module provides students with access to course information, assignments, grades, and communication tools. Students can view their course schedules, submit assignments, participate in discussions, and track their academic progress. Additionally, students can access resources such as lecture notes, readings, and multimedia content.
